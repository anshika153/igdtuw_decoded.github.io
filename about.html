About us

IGDTUW Decoded is your personalized student guide for Indira Gandhi Delhi Technical University for Women (IGDTUW). Founded by students, for students, we aim to make college life easier, more fun, and more productive.

Whether you’re curious about campus hotspots, seeking academic tips, or exploring the best ways to connect and grow, IGDTUW Decoded brings together trusted insights, senior advice, and local hacks—all in one place. We believe that clarity and curiosity go hand-in-hand, and we’re here to make sure every IGDTUW student feels informed, supported, and inspired.

  • Answers to common questions—big and small
  • Community-driven recommendations and resources
